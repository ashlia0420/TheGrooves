About The Grooves

Welcome to The Grooves, the official dance team of College of Engineering, Cherthala. We are a collective of passionate dancers who believe in the power of movement to inspire, express, and unite. From traditional Indian styles to modern hip-hop, our repertoire reflects our commitment to celebrating dance in all its diverse forms.

Since our formation, we’ve set out not just to perform, but to create a community where every student can discover their rhythm, grow in confidence, and find lifelong friendships through dance.

Our Mission

At The Grooves, our mission is to redefine campus dance culture by creating an inclusive, supportive, and innovative environment. We aim to:

  • Empower dancers of all backgrounds to showcase their talent and creativity.
  • Promote a healthy, respectful atmosphere for artistic collaboration.
  • Represent our college with pride at competitions, festivals, and cultural events.
  • Use dance as a medium to break barriers, raise awareness, and spark conversations that matter.

Our Story

It all began with two friends who shared a deep passion for dance and a dream of creating something bigger together. Their countless conversations about rhythm, movement, and teamwork soon inspired them to form a dance crew. With the full support of the institute, they organized auditions to find students who shared their enthusiasm and talent. From this, The Grooves was born — a collective united by dedication and love for the art of dance.

Starting small but driven by steady growth, the crew began performing at college-level events, gradually building their reputation for skill and creativity. Over time, the team has expanded to over 20 talented members, each bringing their unique energy and style.

Today, The Grooves stands as a vibrant and dynamic dance family, led by its two founders and guided by experienced mentorship. Together, they continue to inspire, innovate, and push the boundaries of campus dance culture.
